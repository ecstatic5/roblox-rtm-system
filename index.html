Welcome to Roblox RTM

What's Roblox RTM?

Roblox RTM (Real Time Moderation), is a web application that gives you the ability to manage the moderation of your Roblox game from the web in a quick and easy way!

Locally Hosted

Roblox RTM is hosted locally on your computer, so you don't run the risk of your API-KEY being leaked!

Fast Enough

Roblox RTM is light because of the small number of external packages it uses.

Open-Source

Roblox RTM is a project made by and for the community, so we don't want to make money or pay for it, we just want to help our community!
